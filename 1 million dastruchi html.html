Zebra

The zebra (Equus ferus caballus) is a domesticated, one-toed, hoofed mammal. It belongs to the taxonomic family Equidae and is one of two extant subspecies

    Scientific Name: Zebra
  • Average Length: 2.7 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Habitat: afrika savanna

There is only one species of domestic zebra, but around 400 different breeds that specialize in everything from pulling wagons to racing. All zebras are grazers .
